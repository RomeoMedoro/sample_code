ABOUT US

SuperRestaurant was established by Bob Super in 1970. Mr. Super was a line worker at Ford Motor Company with a great passion for cooking. At the age of 40 he decided to terminate his for employment to pursue his long dream of owning his own restaurant. Today SuperRestaurant has become a popular and well respected restaurant offering delicios dishes at great prices. At SuperRestaurant we care about our customers, and we ensure that all of our ingredients are always fresh, and of the greatest quality. We offer a variety of selections from pizza to high-class seafood dishes. Come try us yourself, we guarantee you'll leave satisfied.
